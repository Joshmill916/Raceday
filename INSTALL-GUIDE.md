# RaceDay — Install & Setup Guide (for tracks)

Welcome to RaceDay. This sheet shows you how to put RaceDay on your device like
a real app, and — most importantly — how to **never lose your data**.

---

## ⚠️ Read this first — protect your data

All of your data (drivers, classes, results, history, license) is stored **inside
your browser on your device**. Two simple rules keep it safe:

1. **Back up before you change anything.** In the app: **Admin → Backup & export →
   Download full backup.** Save that file somewhere safe (email it to yourself).
   Do this at the end of every race day.
2. **NEVER tap "Clear History and Website Data"** in your phone's Safari settings.
   That permanently erases RaceDay's data. (Clearing your *icon* or *reloading* is
   fine — it's only "Clear Website Data" that deletes everything.)

If you ever set up **Multi-device sync** (below), your data also lives safely in the
cloud and these worries mostly go away.

---

## Install RaceDay on your home screen (looks & works like an app)

### iPhone / iPad (use Safari)
1. **Before installing:** open RaceDay in Safari and do **Admin → Download full backup**.
2. Open Safari and go to your RaceDay web address.
3. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up).
4. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**.
5. Launch RaceDay from the new icon. It opens full-screen, like an app.
6. **If the app looks empty** the first time you open it from the icon: that's normal
   on iPhone — the home-screen app starts with its own storage. Just do
   **Admin → Restore from backup** and load the file from step 1. Your data is back.

### Android (use Chrome)
1. Open Chrome and go to your RaceDay web address.
2. Tap the **⋮ menu** (top right) → **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen**).
3. Launch from the new icon — it opens full-screen.

### Laptop / desktop (Chrome or Edge)
1. Open RaceDay in the browser.
2. Click the **install icon** in the address bar (a small monitor/＋ icon), or
   **⋮ menu → Install RaceDay**.
3. It opens in its own window and gets a desktop/Start-menu icon.

## Works offline

After you've opened RaceDay once, it keeps working even if the internet drops at the
track — the app itself loads from the device. (Multi-device sync and the spectator QR
need internet to share live, but the core app runs offline.)

---

## Optional: Multi-device sync (run several devices at once)

Sync lets a registration tablet, a scoring laptop, and a TV display all show the same
live race day — and keeps your data backed up in the cloud.

**To turn it on:**
1. On your main device: **Admin → Multi-device sync → Start sync.**
2. Choose a sync code (your track name works well, e.g. `RIVERSIDE`).
3. On any other device: open RaceDay → **Admin → Multi-device sync → Join** → enter
   the same code. Both devices now stay in step.

Once sync is on, your data is safe across devices and phone changes — just join the
same code on the new device and everything's there.

---

## Activate your license

1. **Admin → License → Activate.**
2. Type the code you were given (looks like `TRACKNAME-S2026-AB12CD`).
3. Done — the trial banner disappears and full features unlock.

Keep your license code somewhere safe. If a device is ever wiped, you just re-enter
the same code to reactivate.

---

## Quick troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| App opened empty after adding to home screen | **Admin → Restore from backup** (this is normal on iPhone) |
| "Logged out" / license gone | **Admin → License → Activate** and re-enter your code |
| Lost data after clearing Safari | Restore your most recent backup file; in future use sync |
| Want it on a second device | Use **Multi-device sync** with the same code |

---

*Always keep a recent backup. It takes 5 seconds and it's the one thing that
guarantees you never lose a season of data.*
